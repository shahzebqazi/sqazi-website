About

Mastering engineer and programmer in Toronto. I build audio software—DSP, plugins, self-hosted music infra, and the agent tooling around it. Music is the career; code is how I ship the weird side projects.

I have a lot of GitHub repos. A handful actually matter—see Projects for the curated list.

Audio. Mixing and mastering, DSP, and gear through Iconoclast Audio and my Reverb shop (DSP pedals and gear). Code: Neck Diagram Studio, Power Ampache 2 plugin template (Android Auto UX at pa2-car-plugin).

AI and agents. Toy Hopfield agent memory (mhn-ai-agent-memory), mystic-ai design showcase (UX demo), public cursor-agents and cursor-config. I primarily use Cursor and care about HCI around a “hand-made feel”.

Math and theory. Category theory, symbolic reasoning, dimensionality reduction, Cauchy sequences, Hilbert spaces, dagger symmetries, and complex analysis—often with audio as the motivating lens. Hopfield-network demo lives under mhn-ai-agent-memory.

Systems. mac-config public Mac patterns (private my-mac-config hub); CachyOS + Cosmic on Strix Halo; Zinwa Q25.

Games. Gankmid Co. Studios.
