About

Mastering engineer and programmer in Toronto. I build audio software—DSP, plugins, self-hosted music infra, and the agent tooling around it. Music is the career; code is how I ship the weird side projects.

I have a lot of GitHub repos. A handful actually matter—see Projects for the curated list.

Audio. Mixing and mastering, DSP, and gear through Iconoclast Audio and my Reverb shop (DSP pedals and gear). Flagship code: Song Pit (Ampache upload flow), PA2 × Android Auto UX research.

AI and agents. Hopfield-network agent memory, search tooling, transcription hotkeys. I primarily use Cursor and care about HCI around a “hand-made feel”—whether feature extraction from that idea could matter for understanding AI agents. Start here: mhn-ai-agent-memory.

Math and theory. Category theory, symbolic reasoning, dimensionality reduction, Cauchy sequences, Hilbert spaces, dagger symmetries, and complex analysis—often with audio as the motivating lens. Demo: hopfield-networks (Haskell + Three.js).

Systems. CachyOS with Cosmic Desktop on an AMD Strix Halo machine, MacBook Pro daily driver, Zinwa Q25, xmonad, AeroSpace, and too many dotfiles. I document the setup in public repos rather than pretending I was born in the kernel.

Games. Gankmid Co. Studios.

Email code@sqazi.sh. Build logs and shorter notes on Mastodon and Matrix; more links on the Links page and Instagram.
